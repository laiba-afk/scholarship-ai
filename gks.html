🇰🇷 GKS Scholarship - South Korea

About

The Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) is a fully funded scholarship offered by the Government of South Korea for international students to study at Korean universities.

Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Meet GKS eligibility requirements
  • ✔ Meet the university admission requirements

Benefits

  • 🎓 Full tuition fee
  • 💰 Monthly stipend
  • 🏥 Health insurance
  • ✈ Round-trip airfare
  • 📚 Korean language training

How to Apply

Visit the official Study in Korea website, choose your program, prepare the required documents, and submit your application before the deadline.
