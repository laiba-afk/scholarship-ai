🇰🇷 GKS Scholarship - South Korea

About

The Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) is a fully funded scholarship offered by the Government of South Korea for international students. It supports undergraduate, master's, doctoral, and research programs at Korean universities.

Benefits

  • ✅ Full Tuition Fee
  • ✅ Monthly Living Allowance
  • ✅ Round-trip Airfare
  • ✅ Medical Insurance
  • ✅ Korean Language Training

Eligibility

  • International students
  • Good academic record
  • Meet GKS eligibility requirements
  • Required educational qualifications
