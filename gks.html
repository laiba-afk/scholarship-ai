🇰🇷 GKS Scholarship - South Korea

About

GKS (Global Korea Scholarship) is a fully funded scholarship offered by the South Korean Government for international students.

Benefits

  • Full Tuition Fee
  • Monthly Stipend
  • Airfare
  • Medical Insurance

Eligibility

  • International students
  • Good academic record
  • Meet GKS program requirements
