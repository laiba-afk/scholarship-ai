🇪🇺 Erasmus Mundus Scholarship

Study in Europe with a Fully Funded Scholarship

📖 About

The Erasmus Mundus Scholarship is a fully funded scholarship funded by the European Union. It allows international students to study master's programs at multiple universities across different European countries.

✅ Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Bachelor's degree (for most programs)
  • ✔ Meet the admission requirements of the selected program

🎁 Benefits

  • 🎓 Full tuition fee
  • 💰 Monthly stipend
  • ✈ Travel allowance
  • 🏠 Living allowance
