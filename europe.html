🇪🇺 Erasmus Mundus Scholarship

Study in Europe with a Fully Funded Scholarship

📖 About

The Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Scholarship is a prestigious, fully funded scholarship sponsored by the European Union. It allows international students to study at multiple universities across Europe and earn a joint or multiple master's degree.

✅ Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Bachelor's degree (for master's programs)
  • ✔ Meet the admission requirements of the selected Erasmus Mundus program
