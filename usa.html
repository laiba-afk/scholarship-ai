🇺🇸 Fulbright Scholarship

Study in USA with a Fully Funded Scholarship

📖 About

Fulbright Scholarship offers fully funded opportunities for international students to study, research, and exchange knowledge in the United States.

✅ Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ English language proficiency
  • ✔ Meet university admission requirements

🎁 Benefits

  • 🎓 Tuition fee support
  • 💰 Monthly stipend
  • 🏥 Health insurance
  • ✈ Travel support

📄 Required Documents

  • 📄 Passport
  • 📄 Academic Transcripts
  • 📄 CV / Resume
  • 📄 Statement of Purpose
  • 📄 Recommendation Letter

📝 How to Apply

1. Visit the official Fulbright website.
2. Check eligibility criteria.
3. Prepare required documents.
4. Submit your application before the deadline.
