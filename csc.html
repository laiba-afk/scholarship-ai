🇨🇳 CSC Scholarship - China

About

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) offers fully funded scholarships for international students to study at top universities in China.

Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Meet university admission requirements
  • ✔ English or Chinese language proficiency (if required)

Benefits

  • 🎓 Full tuition fee
  • 💰 Monthly stipend
  • 🏠 Free accommodation
  • 🏥 Medical insurance

How to Apply

Visit the official CSC website, choose a university, prepare the required documents, and submit your application before the deadline.
