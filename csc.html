🇨🇳 CSC Scholarship

Study in China with a Fully Funded Scholarship

📖 About

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is a fully funded scholarship offered by the Government of China for international students to study at top Chinese universities.

✅ Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Meet the university admission requirements
  • ✔ English or Chinese language proficiency (if required)

🎁 Benefits

  • 🎓 Full tuition fee
  • 💰 Monthly stipend
  • 🏠 Free accommodation
  • 🏥 Comprehensive medical insurance
  • 📚 Study at top Chinese universities

📄 Required Documents

  • 📄 Passport
  • 📄 Academic Transcripts
  • 📄 Study Plan
  • 📄 Recommendation Letters
  • 📄 Language Certificate (if required)

📝 How to Apply

1. Visit the official CSC website.
2. Choose your preferred university.
3. Prepare all required documents.
4. Complete the online application.
5. Submit your application before the deadline.
