🇨🇳 CSC Scholarship - China

About

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is a fully funded scholarship for international students to study at Chinese universities.

Benefits

  • 🎓 Full Tuition Fee
  • 💰 Monthly Stipend
  • 🏠 Free Accommodation
  • 🏥 Medical Insurance

Eligibility

  • ✔ International students
  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Meet university admission requirements

How to Apply

Visit the official CSC website, choose a university, prepare your documents, and submit your application before the deadline.
