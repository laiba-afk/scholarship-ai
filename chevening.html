📖 About

Chevening Scholarships are fully funded scholarships offered by the UK Government for outstanding international students to pursue a one-year master's degree at universities in the United Kingdom.

✅ Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Meet Chevening scholarship requirements
  • ✔ Meet university admission requirements

🎁 Benefits

  • 🎓 Full tuition fee
  • 💰 Monthly living allowance
  • ✈ Round-trip airfare
  • 🏠 Arrival allowance
  • 🌍 Opportunity to study at top UK universities

📄 Required Documents

  • 📄 Passport
  • 📄 Academic Transcripts
  • 📄 CV / Resume
  • 📄 Recommendation Letters
  • 📄 University Offer Letter (if required)
