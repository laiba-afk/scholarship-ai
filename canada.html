🇨🇦 Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarship

Study in Canada with a Prestigious Scholarship

📖 About

The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships (Vanier CGS) program is one of Canada's most prestigious scholarships. It supports outstanding international and Canadian doctoral students at leading Canadian universities.

✅ Eligibility

  • ✔ Excellent academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ Must be nominated by a Canadian university
  • ✔ Meet Vanier CGS eligibility requirements

🎁 Benefits

  • 🎓 Scholarship worth CAD $50,000 per year
  • 📅 Funding for up to 3 years
  • 🏫 Study at top Canadian universities
  • 🌍 International research opportunities

📄 Required Documents

  • 📄 Passport
  • 📄 Academic Transcripts
  • 📄 Research Proposal
  • 📄 CV / Resume
