🇯🇵 MEXT Scholarship - Japan

About

MEXT (Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology) is a fully funded scholarship offered by the Government of Japan for international students.

Benefits

  • Full Tuition Fee
  • Monthly Stipend
  • Round-trip Airfare
  • Preparatory Japanese Language Training (if required)

Eligibility

  • International students
  • Excellent academic record
  • Meet MEXT eligibility requirements
