🇩🇪 DAAD Scholarship

About

DAAD (German Academic Exchange Service) offers fully funded scholarships for international students to study in Germany.

Eligibility

  • ✔ Good academic record
  • ✔ Valid passport
  • ✔ English or German language proficiency (if required)
  • ✔ Meet the university admission requirements

Benefits

  • 🎓 Full tuition support
  • 💰 Monthly stipend
  • 🏥 Health insurance
  • ✈ Travel allowance (for some programs)

How to Apply

Visit the official DAAD website, choose your course, prepare the required documents, and submit your application before the deadline.
