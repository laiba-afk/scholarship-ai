🇹🇷 Türkiye Scholarships

About

Türkiye Scholarships is a fully funded scholarship offered by the Government of Türkiye for international students.

Benefits

  • Full Tuition Fee
  • Monthly Stipend
  • Free Accommodation
  • Health Insurance
  • Round-trip Airfare

Eligibility

  • International students
  • Good academic record
  • Meet Türkiye Scholarships requirements
